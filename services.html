Frequently Asked Questions

Questions About Our Services

What is brand strategy and why do I need it?

Brand strategy defines your brand's purpose, positioning, values, and target audience. It answers fundamental questions: why does your brand exist, who is it for, and what makes it different? Without a solid strategy, visual identity becomes decoration rather than communication. Our research & strategy services ensure every design decision is backed by insight.

How does AI integration work in branding projects?

We integrate AI across the branding process to accelerate workflows and enhance outcomes. This includes AI-powered market research, automated competitor analisys, AI marketing automation setup, and team training to use AI tools effectively. Our goal is to make your brand AI-ready from day one.

What is a brandbook and why is it essential?

A brandbook (or brand guidelines) is a comprehensive document that establishes how your brand should look, sound, and behave across all channels. It includes logo usage rules, color palettes, typography systems, photography styles, tone of voice guidelines, and dos and don'ts. It ensures brand consistency as your team grows and as more external partners touch your brand.

How do you approach naming a new brand?

Our naming process combines linguistic analysis, cultural screening, trademark availability checks, and domain availability verification. We generate hundreds of candidate names, then systematically evaluate them against criteria like memorability, meaning, distinctiveness, and global appropriateness before presenting our top recommendations.

What deliverables do I receive at the end of a branding project?

Depending on the scope, deliverables typically include: brand strategy document, visual identity system (logo files in multiple formats, color specifications, typography guidelines), brandbook, verbal identity materials, template files, and AI integration documentation. All assets are provided in production-ready formats with full usage rights.
