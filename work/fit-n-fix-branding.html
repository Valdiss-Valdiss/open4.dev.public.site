Fit-N-Fix

Premium backpack suspension systems engineered for comfort and reliability on every adventure

Project Overview

Creating a brand that elevates backpack suspension systems from generic components to professional-grade solutions. Fit-N-Fix delivers the comfort and reliability that discerning outdoor enthusiasts demand — ensuring every Kanrock backpack becomes a trusted companion on any journey.

Services Naming, Visual Identity
Sectors Sporting Goods, Outdoor Equipment, Camping Gear
Client Kant / SportBrandDevelopment
Team Vlad Volny, Anton Danilov

The Challenge

A backpack is only as good as its suspension system — the straps, harnesses, and attachments that distribute weight and prevent chafing on long treks. Yet most manufacturers treat suspension as an afterthought. Kanrock needed a branded suspension system that would elevate their backpacks above the competition.

The task: develop Fit-N-Fix as a premium branded suspension system that communicates quality and attention to detail — transforming a technical component into a marketed feature.

How We Did It

Naming: Developed the Fit-N-Fix brand name that clearly communicates functionality: "fit" for adjustability, "fix" for secure attachment. A system for quick adjustment and secure fixation of backpack suspension.

Visual Identity: Created a premium label and branding elements that integrate seamlessly with Kanrock's design language — transforming a technical component into a marketed feature that customers can trust.

The Solution and the Impact

Fit-N-Fix suspension systems are now a signature feature of Kanrock backpacks. The branded suspension enhances the overall product offering, communicating to customers that every detail — even what you can't see — has been thoughtfully engineered.

Results: Fit-N-Fix has become an integral part of the Kanrock brand ecosystem, elevating their backpacks with a recognizable, trusted suspension component.
