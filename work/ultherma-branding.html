Ultherma

Premium sleeping bag insulation that adds invisible value to every Kanrock product

Project Overview

Creating a brand that elevates sleeping bag insulation from a generic component to a statement of premium craftsmanship. Ultherma demonstrates that true quality lies in the details you don't see — yet feel with every use.

Services Naming, Visual Identity
Sectors Sporting Goods, Outdoor Equipment, Camping Gear
Client Kant / SportBrandDevelopment
Team Vlad Volny, Anton Danilov

The Challenge

Premium products require premium details — even those invisible to the eye. Kanrock needed an insulation brand that would add perceived value to their sleeping bags, signaling to customers that every component has been thoughtfully considered and crafted.

The task: develop Ultherma as a distinctive brand that reinforces Kanrock's commitment to quality at every level — even in components customers never directly see.

How We Did It

Brand Development: Developed Ultherma as a distinctive brand within the Kanrock ecosystem. Created a premium label design that adds tangible value to the product — communicating that this sleeping bag contains something special inside.

Design Process: Three variants were explored, but the chosen design best harmonized with the Kanrock brand identity — modern, technological, and clean. The letter "T" in the logo subtly evokes the concept of temperature, while the cross intersection of horizontal and vertical lines resembles a plus sign, symbolizing warmth and comfort.

The Solution and the Impact

Ultherma insulation is now an integral part of Kanrock's premium product line. The branded insulation enhances the overall perception of quality, reinforcing that Kanrock spare no effort in crafting their products — even in components customers never directly see.

Results: Ultherma adds invisible value that customers feel — a testament to Kanrock's commitment to premium quality at every level of their products.
