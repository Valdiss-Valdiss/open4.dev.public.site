Kanrock Catalog

International-level product catalog showcasing premium outdoor equipment for the global market

Project Overview

Kanrock is a world-class outdoor gear brand that inspires you to discover the world. The brand offers a comprehensive range of trekking equipment including backpacks, sleeping bags, and sleeping mats — all designed for the most demanding outdoor enthusiasts.

The task was to create a product catalog that would present the full range of Kanrock products to an international audience. The catalog needed to be informative, visually compelling, and meet the high standards of the global outdoor equipment market.

Services Catalog Design, Product Photography, Copy
Sectors Sporting Goods, Outdoor Equipment, Tourism
Client Kant / SportBrandDevelopment
Website kanrock.com
Team Vlad Volny, Ekaterina Umasheva

The Challenge

The challenge was to create a product catalog that would effectively communicate the quality, functionality, and reliability of Kanrock equipment to both trade partners (B2B) and end consumers (B2C).

Inform and Educate — Present detailed product specifications, materials, and features in a clear, accessible format.

Visual Appeal — Showcase products with professional photography and modern design.

International Standards — Meet the expectations of the global outdoor equipment market.

Comprehensive Coverage — Present the full product range including PRO and TECH lines.

The catalog needed to work for distributors, retailers, and end customers across multiple countries, communicating consistent brand values of reliability, quality, and adventure.

How We Did It

Product Range Organization: The catalog was structured to showcase Kanrock's comprehensive product lines:

PRO Line — For the most experienced users, featuring the latest and most functional materials from world's leading manufacturers.

TECH Line — Equipment for active lifestyle lovers, with optimal combination of materials and modern manufacturing processes.

Product Categories Featured:

Backpacks — Eclipse, Cruiser, Moveit, Charger, Vector, Prostep, Groove (trekking, hiking, and urban models).

Sleeping Bags — Axiom and Aurora ranges (with Fellex and UlTherma insulation).

Sleeping Mats — Airlive and Dreamer Mat (air and self-inflating options).

Design Approach: Clean, modern layout with emphasis on product photography and technical specifications. Each product includes detailed features list, materials, weights, dimensions, and color options.

Information Architecture: Logical flow from brand story (Mission, Values) through product lines to detailed specifications — helping customers understand not just what they're buying, but why Kanrock is the right choice.

The Solution and the Impact

What was delivered: A comprehensive product catalog that presents Kanrock's full range of outdoor equipment at an international level. The catalog successfully balances visual appeal with detailed technical information, helping customers understand product benefits and make informed decisions.

Results: The Kanrock catalog was recognized by the professional sports community as meeting the standards of established international outdoor brands. It serves as a key sales tool for the brand's expansion into international markets.
