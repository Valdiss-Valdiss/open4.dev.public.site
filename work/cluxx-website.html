CLUXX

Modern multilingual website showcasing premium bike accessories for adventurers worldwide

Project Overview

CLUXX is a premium bicycle accessories brand offering pedals, bottles, locks, fenders, and more for cyclists who demand reliability and style. The brand needed a modern digital presence that reflects its international ambitions and positions CLUXX alongside leading cycling brands in both quality and visual appeal.

Services Web Design, Product Photography, Copy
Sectors Sporting Goods, Cycling
Client Kant / SportBrandDevelopment
Websitecluxx.com
TeamVlad Volny, Ildar Zinnyatullov

The Challenge

With a strong product lineup including the popular Ergo bottles, Gardia locks, and Rapid pedals series, CLUXX needed a website that could effectively communicate brand values of reliability and functionality to a global audience.

The challenge was to create a digital presence that matches the premium quality of the products.

How We Did It

Research & Strategy: Analyzed the cycling accessories market and CLUXX target audience to understand how cyclists evaluate equipment reliability and brand trust. Key insight: cyclists need to feel confident their accessories won't fail during rides.

UX/UI Design: Built a modern multilingual website with emphasis on large product photography and intuitive navigation. Key sections include product catalogs with detailed specs, dealer locator for B2B partners, and content that communicates the brand mission of "Reliable and functional bike accessories for unforgettable adventures."

Multi-language Support: Created responsive design that works seamlessly across devices for cyclists on the go. The site supports multiple languages for international expansion.

The Solution and the Impact

The new CLUXX website successfully launched with full product catalogs in multiple languages. The modern design and intuitive user experience helped establish CLUXX as a premium international brand.

What was delivered: A fully responsive multilingual website with detailed product catalogs, dealer locator, and content strategy that communicates brand values of reliability and functionality.

Results: The website provides customers and dealers with all the information they need to make informed decisions about CLUXX accessories.
