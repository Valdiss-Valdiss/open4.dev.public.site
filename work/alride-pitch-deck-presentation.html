Alride Pitch Deck

Engineering a high-conversion investment narrative to scale a regional leader into a global sports retail powerhouse

Project Overview

Alride, a sports retail leader with over 10 years of market dominance, needed to transition from a successful regional player to an international investment-ready venture. We developed a strategic pitch deck designed to secure co-investment for their global expansion. By combining high-end design with conversion-focused storytelling, we transformed complex business data into a compelling opportunity for institutional and private investors.

Services Pitch Deck Design, Copy
Sectors Sports Retail, Venture Capital, International Business
Client Alride
Team Vlad Volny, Ildar Zinnyatullov

The Challenge

Despite a strong track record and an 18% growth forecast, Alride's existing materials lacked the "premium" feel required to attract world-class partners. The challenge was to articulate their multi-faceted expertise—spanning retail, distribution, and resort management—into a clear, unified vision that could justify a major international scale-up.

How We Did It

We built a brand-new visual and narrative identity for Alride's international debut. Starting with a blank canvas, our team translated a decade of market leadership into a dynamic, data-driven presentation that captures the energy of the sports industry. We focused on highlighting key growth drivers—such as their expansive influencer network and omnichannel sales expertise—to ensure the pitch felt like a premium, world-class invitation to a strategic partnership.

The Solution and the Impact

The result was a world-class presentation that successfully bridged the gap between Alride's heritage and its future global mission. The pitch deck effectively resonated with the target audience: it facilitated immediate interest from the investment community, resulting in the acquisition of multiple potential co-investors ready to fuel Alride's international journey.
